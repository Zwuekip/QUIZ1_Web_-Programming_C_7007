Hometown

Jayapura

My hometown is Jayapura,the capital city of Papua province, and the place where I was born and raised there. For me living there is very pleasant, because as a teenager, I often explore the forest at my school. the most beautiful memory in Jayapura for me is when night falls and the lights throughout the city go out, and when that moment occurs, the moonlight will illuminate the night and the sky will looks very beautiful. I hope you can go to my hometown and feel the happiness I feel there.
